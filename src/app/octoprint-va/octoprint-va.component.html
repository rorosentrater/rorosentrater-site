Octoprint voice assistant

Amazon Alexa is a voice assistant that, on top of it's default features, has the ability to learn new "skills". If you didn't know, there is a sort of app store for Alexa. I have written and published a skill to the Amazon store called "Octoprint voice assistant" that you can download and use with Alexa today. What is the Octoprint voice assistant you might ask? Well it makes use of the popular 3D printing management software Octoprint. If you connect your printer to a computer (nothing fancy, most people use Raspberry PIs) you can download and install this software to manage, monitor, and remotely access your 3D printer. My skill takes advantage of the Octoprint APIs and allows you to use certain octoprint features WITH YOUR VOICE
